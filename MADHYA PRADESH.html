Dhol
+

The Dhol is a large, two-headed drum commonly used in folk music and celebrations across India, especially in states like Madhya Pradesh, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, and others. It is often played in weddings, festivals, and rituals, and is an important instrument in the Garba and Dandiya dances of Madhya Pradesh.

Its thunderous sound energizes crowds and sets the tempo for lively dances. It is also played during **weddings** and **rituals**, adding to the festive atmosphere.
